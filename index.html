Our Story

Healing Uvuru, One Patient at a Time

John-Theresa Spiritan Specialist Hospital Uvuru was founded with a sacred mission: to bring world-class healthcare to the heart of the community. Rooted in Spiritan values of service and solidarity, we exist to heal without discrimination.

Our facility is currently expanding — a testament to the growing trust of the Uvuru community and our commitment to meeting every healthcare need at home, without the burden of travelling far for specialist care.

  • ✓ Spiritan mission-driven care rooted in compassion
  • ✓ Experienced specialists across key medical disciplines
  • ✓ Modern diagnostic and surgical facilities
  • ✓ Accessible, affordable care for all community members
  • ✓ Ongoing infrastructure expansion for greater capacity
